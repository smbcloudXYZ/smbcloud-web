[smbCloud](https://smbcloud.xyz) offers small and medium-sized businesses easy, on-demand access to powerful, open-source software solutions in the cloud—without the hassle of installation or expensive licenses.

## Our Mission

At smbCloud, our mission is to democratize access to modern cloud infrastructure and developer tools. We believe that every business, regardless of size, should have the ability to deploy, scale, and manage applications with the same ease and power as large enterprises.

## What We Offer

- **Unified Platform:** Deploy NodeJS (with NextJS or Astro), Swift (via Vapor), and Ruby (on Rails) apps with a single command.
- **No Vendor Lock-in:** Built on open standards and open-source technologies, giving you full control over your stack.
- **Instant Collaboration:** Real-time team collaboration and sharing, with secure access controls.
- **Developer Delight:** Fast feedback, intuitive UI, and seamless integrations with GitHub and other tools.
- **Enterprise-Grade Security:** Automatic HTTPS, secure authentication, and best practices by default.

## Why Choose smbCloud?

- **Simplicity:** Get started in minutes—no complex setup or infrastructure management required.
- **Flexibility:** Scale from a single project to multiple teams and environments effortlessly.
- **Transparency:** Open source at its core, with a commitment to community-driven development.
- **Support:** Dedicated to helping small and medium businesses succeed in the cloud era.
