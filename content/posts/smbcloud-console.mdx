We’re excited to announce the public beta release of our [smbCloud Console](https://console.smbcloud.xyz)!

## What is the smbCloud Console?

The smbCloud Console is a unified cloud management platform designed specifically for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). With an intuitive interface and powerful automation, it streamlines your deployment workflow and makes managing cloud resources effortless.

### Key Features

- **GitHub Integration**: Connect your GitHub account to deploy your projects with just a few clicks.
- **One-Click Deployments**: Launch NodeJS, Swift (Vapor), and Ruby (Rails) apps instantly.
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: Track your deployments, logs, and resource usage in real time.
- **Team Collaboration**: Invite team members and manage permissions with ease.
- **Secure by Default**: Built-in best practices for authentication and data protection.

## Get Started

Ready to try it out? [Sign in to the console](https://console.smbcloud.xyz), connect your GitHub account, and start deploying your apps today. We welcome your feedback as we continue to improve the platform—let us know what features you’d like to see next!
